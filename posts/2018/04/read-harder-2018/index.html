(Read Harder) Challenge accepted

Back in mid-December, BookRiot posted its 2018 Read Harder Challenge. This is the fourth round of the challenge, which gives 24 tasks to expand one's reading horizons.

I'm a bit late to getting started on it, though I happened to have read at least one thing that qualifies (Ursula K. LeGuin's The Left Hand of Darkness, at around the time of her death, completely by coincidence).

This is also the first year I found out about it. With the pile of reading I have already amassed for myself, I doubt I'll get to all 24. But I'm going to add some from the list to what I read this year.

In other words: challenge accepted!
